Sam Ticknor is a multidisciplinary artist located in New York City (previously, Pittsburgh, PA). Sam spent many years making art in Pittsburgh at Carnegie Mellon University where she earned a BFA in Fine Art, at the Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh where she worked as a Creative Technologist, at Union Project where she was a member of the ceramics cooperative, and in her home studio. These days, Sam is focusing on painting colorful vignettes of the moments that stick in her memory.
