Bio
Sam Ticknor is a multidisciplinary artist living in New York City. Sam previously lived and worked in Pittsburgh, PA. There, she earned a BFA in Fine Art at Carnegie Mellon University, worked as a Creative Technologist at the Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh, and was a member of the Union Project cermaics cooperative.

Statement
Lately, I have been focused on making new paintings. This body of work is largely figurative, featuring scenes that have one foot in reality and one foot in my imagination. The scenery is adapted from my modern life and steeped in metaphor. My reverence for humanity and nature is revealed through tender storytelling about my paintings’ subjects. I draw viewers in with curious optical play and entice them to make sense of the narrative. My punchlines range from comedic to bittersweet to alarming.
